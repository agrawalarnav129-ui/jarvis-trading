### Hard Rules (NEVER OVERRIDE)

**Regime Rules**:
- BULLISH: Nifty > EMA50 > EMA200, ADX > 20 → Full position size (2% risk)
- NEUTRAL: ADX 15–20 or between EMAs → Half position (1% risk), R:R ≥ 2.5:1
- BEARISH: Nifty < EMA50, EMA50 < EMA200 → No long entries

**Screener Filters** (All Must Pass):
- MIN_AVG_VOLUME: 500,000 shares/day
- MIN_PRICE: ₹50
- MIN_ATR: 1% daily
- MAX_ATR: 6% daily
- MIN_ADX: 18
- EMA_ALIGNMENT: EMA9 > EMA21 > EMA50 > EMA200
- ABOVE_EMA200: Required
- VOLUME_CONFIRM: ≥ 1.5× 20-day average

**Risk Management** (Absolute Limits):
- Stop Loss: Never wider than 2% of capital
- Max Positions: 2 open trades simultaneously
- Min R:R: 2:1 (NEUTRAL: 2.5:1)
- Daily Max Drawdown: 4–5% → Hard stop on new entries
- Position Sizing: `(Capital × Risk%) / (Entry - Stop)`

**Entry Rejection Checklist**:
- [ ] Setup grade is C → REJECT
- [ ] Regime is BEARISH (long entry) → REJECT
- [ ] Stop would force risk > 2% of capital → REJECT
- [ ] Already 2 positions open → REJECT (watchlist only)
- [ ] Daily P&L ≤ -4% → REJECT (hard stop)
- [ ] Volume confirmation < 1.5× → REJECT
- [ ] ADX < 18 → REJECT
- [ ] Entry during 9:15–9:30 AM IST → REJECT (avoid first 15min)
- [ ] Entering because "already moved a lot" → FOMO REJECT

## Screener Configuration

### Grade System (A / B / C)

**Grade A (≥75 points)**: Strong Buy
- Price Action: 20+ (clear structure)
- Relative Strength: 20+ (leading sector)
- Volume: 18+ (bullish accumulation)
- Trend: 18+ (strong ADX, aligned EMAs)
- **Action**: Watchlist-eligible, execute on confirmation

**Grade B (55–74 points)**: Watchlist / Monitor
- Mixed signals, wait for further confirmation
- **Action**: Add to watchlist, wait for price action confirmation before entry

**Grade C (<55 points)**: Ignore
- **Action**: Do not present as trading opportunity

### Scoring Weights
- Price Action: 35% (0–35 points)
- Relative Strength: 25% (0–25 points)
- Volume Quality: 20% (0–20 points)
- Trend Strength: 20% (0–20 points)

## Workflow Decisions

### When to Add New Symbols to Watchlist
1. Must pass screener Grade A or B
2. Volume > 500K avg daily
3. Price > ₹50
4. ADX ≥ 18
5. Above EMA200 (bullish bias)
6. Relative strength positive vs sector

### When to Execute a Trade
1. Regime is BULLISH or NEUTRAL (never BEARISH longs)
2. Setup grade is A or B
3. Breakout confirmed on volume ≥ 1.5× 20-day avg
4. Stop loss honors 2% capital risk limit
5. R:R ≥ 2:1 (NEUTRAL: 2.5:1)
6. Time: Avoid 9:15–9:30 AM (avoid first 15 min gap)
7. Psychological check: No FOMO, conviction ≥ 7/10

### When to Move Stop to Breakeven
- Position at +1R (1 Risk unit profit)
- Lock in the R:R floor

### When to Trailing Stop at 1.5× ATR
- Position at +2R (2 Risk units profit)
- Trail at 1.5× 14-period ATR

### When to Exit
- Stop hit → Automatic exit, log outcome
- Target 1 hit → Hold for Target 2 with trailed stop
- Target 2 hit → Full exit, record win
- Invalidation signal → Exit on structure break
- Max hold 5 days → Exit or reassess (avoid overnight hold past expiry)
